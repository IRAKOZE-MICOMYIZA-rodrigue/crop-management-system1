# Crop Management System - Presentation Content

## Slide 1: Title Slide
**Title:** Crop Management System
**Subtitle:** A Comprehensive Agricultural Tracking Solution

## Slide 2: Problem Statement
**Real-World Problem:**
- Farmers struggle with manual crop tracking
- Data loss and poor harvest planning
- Lack of centralized management system
- Inefficient sales and inventory tracking

## Slide 3: Solution Overview
**Key Features:**
- Digital crop lifecycle tracking
- User role management (Farmers & Admin)
- Harvest logging and sales recording
- Real-time notifications using Observer pattern
